\yh{在这部名为《劝导》的漫画中，我们看到了一个存在于我们日常生活中的现象：一个在公共汽车上找不到座位的老人正在努力平衡自己。一些年轻人没有给他让座，而是一动不动地坐在那里取笑他。一位男士说:“考虑到你的年龄，你不应该乘公共汽车。对你来说太挤了!然后一个年轻女人附和道:“是的，你应该下车打车!”}{In this comic entitled persuasion, we see a phenomenon which exists in our everyday life: an old man who can’t find a seat in the bus is striving hard to balance himself. Instead of offering their seats to him, some of the young people just sit there motionlessly and make fun of him. A man says: “considering your age, you shouldn’t take the bus. It’s too crowded for you!” Then a young woman echoes: “yes, you should get off the bus and take a taxi!”
}
\yh{这幅画以讽刺的口吻批评了那些不尊重老人的年轻人，并表达了对老人的关心。老年人是我们社会的一个重要组成部分。他们为社会的发展做出了毕生的贡献。我们年轻人非常感谢他们所做的一切和牺牲。虽然他们现在身体还不强壮，但我们不应该忘记他们的贡献而变得冷酷无情。此外，我们总有一天也会变老。如果我们不给下一代树立一个好榜样，维护中国悠久的尊重老人的传统，那时谁来照顾我们呢?站在他们的角度想想，如果我们生活在这样一个冷漠的社会，我们会怎么做?}{With an ironic tone, the picture criticizes those disrespectful young people and shows concern for the old one. Old people are an important part of our society. They have contributed all their life to the society’s development. We young people owe a lot to them for what they have done and sacrificed. Though now they are not physically strong, we should not forget their contributions and become unmerciful. Besides, we will also get old one day. If we don’t set a good example for the later generation and preserve China’s long-established tradition of respecting the old, who will take care of us at that time? To put ourselves into their shoes, how would we if we live in such an indifferent society?}
\yh{总而言之，我们应该尊重老人，在我们力所能及的时候照顾他们。他们是社会的财富，而不是负担。只有我们把这个高尚的传统传承下去，我们的社会才能成为一个更好的社会。}{To sum up, we should have respect for the old people and take care of them whenever we can. They are a treasure to the society, not a burden. Only if we carry this noble tradition on can our society becomes a better community.}
}
